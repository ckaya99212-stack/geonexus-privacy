Introduction This Privacy Policy describes how KayaMobile Studio handles information in the GeoNexus mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. 2. Data Collection and Use GeoNexus is designed as a tool for exploring global data. Personal Data: We do not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as your name, address, or phone number). Location Data: If you grant permission, the app may use your approximate location to calculate distances to countries. This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers. 3. Advertising and Third-Party Services This application contains advertisements. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies and/or device identifiers to serve ads based on your interests. Ad Partners: We may use partners like Google AdMob. These providers may collect data such as your IP address, device ID, and usage patterns to show relevant ads. External APIs: GeoNexus uses third-party services to provide functionality: RestCountries API: For country data. Open-Meteo: For live weather and air quality. Frankfurter: For currency exchange rates. Wikipedia: For summary descriptions. These services have their own privacy policies regarding how they handle data. 4. Cookies Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. While the app itself does not use "first-party cookies," third-party ad networks may use them to track ad performance. 5. Children’s Privacy Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. 6.
